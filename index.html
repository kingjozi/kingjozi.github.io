About Me

I am a dedicated and versatile biomedical engineer with a proven track record in biomaterial design. My foundation lies in the intricate realm of biomaterials, where I have honed my skills in designing materials that are compatible with the human body as well as drug loading and release profile studies on biomaterials. My journey in the field has been marked by commitment, constantly seeking new challenges and opportunities to make a meaningful impact on patient care.
While my roots descend from biomaterials, my curiosity has led me to explore new research frontiers in two exciting realms - computer vision and biomechanics. Fascinated by the intricate interplay between the human body, and mechanical forces I am actively engaged in research aimed at improving performance.
In addition to research, I am eloquent public speaker. I am also an independent and creative thinker with a sincere desire learn, connect with experts, contribute to research and help provide solutions to the problems of this world.
